Beignets

Tiana's beignets from the princess and the frog

Ingredients

  • 1/2 teaspoon dry yeast
  • 1/4 cup warm water (105°)
  • 1/4 cup sugar
  • 2 tablespoons vegetable shortening
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 1/2 cup heavy cream
  • 1 egg
  • 4 cups all-purpose flour
  • 1/2 cup boiling water
  • Vegetable oil for frying
  • Powdered sugar
    •

    Steps

    1. Sprinkle yeast over warm water in a small bowl. Stir to dissolve.
    2. Add sugar, shortening, salt, heavy cream, egg, flour, yeast and boiling water to a large bowl. Mix together.
    3. Rest dough mixture for 30 minutes. Cover with a towel.
    4. Roll out dough to 1/4-inch thickness and cut into individual squares. Let dough rise for two hours in a warm area. Again, you can cover with a dish towel.
    5. Heat vegetable oil to 350º and fry beignets for two to three minutes, until golden brown. I usually only do two or three at a time, flipping them once or twice for an even fry.
    6. Dust warm beignets with powdered sugar.

    Enjoy!
