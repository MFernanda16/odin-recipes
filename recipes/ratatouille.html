Ratatouille

Ratatouille from Ratatouille

Ingredients

  • 2 eggplants
  • 3 big tomatoes
  • 1 bell pepper
  • 2 zucchinis
  • 1 onion
  • 3 tablespoons of olive oil
  • Mixed herbs
  • 2 cloves pressed garlic
  • Salt
  • Pepper

Steps

  1. Wash and slice the zucchinis, eggplants, bell peppers, and tomatoes, and then slice the onions.
  2. Arrange the sliced vegetables in an alternating pattern in a single layer in an oven-safe dish.
  3. Drizzle the vegetables with olive oil and season with the herbs, salt, and pepper according to your taste.
  4. Bake in the oven at 350 degrees Fahrenheit for 30-35 minutes. 10 minutes before the end of the time, add your two cloves of pressed garlic and allow them to bake for the rest of the time.

Voila!
